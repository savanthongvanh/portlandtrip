## Chicago stop details

### Best Chicago Thai choice

**Star of Siam - River North**  
This is my pick now that you're allowing more Chicago time. It is more Thai-focused than Hot Woks Cool Sushi and still reasonably close to the Riverwalk / Millennium Park area.

Suggested Chicago flow:

1. Park near **Millennium Park** or **River North**.
2. Walk **Cloud Gate / The Bean -> Crown Fountain -> Riverwalk**.
3. Eat at **Star of Siam**.
4. Leave Chicago by **1:30 pm CT**.

### If parking simplicity matters more

Use **Hot Woks Cool Sushi near Michigan Ave** instead. It is less "destination Thai," but it keeps the lunch stop simpler and closer to Millennium Park.
