## Chicago stop details

### Best Chicago Thai choice

**Star of Siam - River North**  
This is my pick now that you're allowing more Chicago time. It is more Thai-focused than Hot Woks Cool Sushi and still reasonably close to the Riverwalk / Millennium Park area.

Suggested Chicago flow:

1. Park in an accessible garage near **Millennium Park** or **River North**.
2. Do a short, step-free route: **Cloud Gate / The Bean -> Crown Fountain -> Riverwalk access point**.
3. Eat at **Star of Siam** with confirmed accessible seating/restroom.
4. Leave Chicago by **1:30 pm CT**.

### If parking simplicity matters more

Use **Hot Woks Cool Sushi near Michigan Ave** instead. It is less "destination Thai," but it can keep the lunch stop simpler with shorter walking from Millennium Park.
